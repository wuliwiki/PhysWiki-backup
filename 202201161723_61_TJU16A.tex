$\quad (2)\quad (AB+BA)^{\dagger} = B^*A^*+A^*+B^* = AB+BA $，是厄米算符． \\

$\quad (5)\quad [i(AB-BA)]^{\dagger} = -i(BA-AB) = i(AB-BA) $，是厄密算符．
